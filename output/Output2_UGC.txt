Strategy Notes for Success:
Authenticity: For these videos, use your phone’s native camera. Do not use over-produced lighting; it makes it look like a corporate ad, which kills conversion rates for UGC.

Pacing: Keep the edits snappy. In all three platforms, never go more than 3 seconds without a visual change (angle change, text overlay, or movement).

Placement: On Instagram, use "Text-to-Speech" for the hooks to improve retention. On Facebook, ensure the first 3 lines of your primary text grab attention, as the rest will be hidden behind a "See More" button.
